Introducing my school

City Model College.

A French, Arabic and English Speaking School. A mixed learning school.

The name of my school is City Model College. Located in the vibrant city of Port Harcourt, Nigeria, City Model College stands as a beacon of academic excellence, offering a unique blend of Nigerian and British educational values. As a premier institution, they proudly operate a British curriculum, designed to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and global perspectives.

Our Facilities

- Modern classrooms and laboratories.
- Well-stocked library and resource center.
- Sports facilities, including football pitches, basketball courts, and tennis courts.
- A well built mosque with proper facilities.
- Computer labs and IT infrastructure.

Accomplishments and Achievements

Our students have consistently demonstrated exceptional performance in national and international examinations, including:
- Outstanding results in IGCSE and A-Level exams.

Conclusion

City Model College remains committed to providing a holistic education that prepares students for success in all aspects of life. By combining the best of Nigerian and British educational traditions, we empower our students to become confident, responsible, and global citizens.
